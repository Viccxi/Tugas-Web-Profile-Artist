About Me

Hi, I'm Ovic — known as Viccxi. I'm an independent artist driven purely by passion for music. I create because it’s in my heart, and I hope my songs resonate with the people who hear them.

I started my music journey in 2021 with my debut single "Hey See Me". From that point on, I’ve kept creating, growing, and evolving. Every aspect of my songs — from the lyrics, arrangement, composition, beatmaking, to mixing and mastering — has been done entirely by me. This is my craft, and I pour my soul into every piece.

Between 2021 and 2025, I’ve released four singles as a solo artist and one collaboration single with the band Bitternight, titled "Lepaskan". I do all of this without any financial return, but I keep going — because music is who I am.

I’m beyond grateful for everyone who has supported me, listened to my music, and walked with me on this journey. Your presence means the world to me. And no matter what, I will keep creating.

– Viccxi
